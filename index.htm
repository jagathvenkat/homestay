Services

1. A 4 bedroom well furnished house with a spacious hall/dining room.
2. All the bedrooms contain a double cot with well maintained bedsheets, blankets and pillows, a TV, dressing mirror, a table fan, a cloth stand.
3. Bedrooms have attached bathrooms wid 24/7 hot water service(geaser) which is a must for the constant cold weather.
